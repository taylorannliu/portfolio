Hi, I’m Taylor, an aspiring designer and illustrator whose specialty is using bright colours and fantastical concepts to create whimsical design work. Born and raised in a family of fishermen in Vancouver, BC, I enjoy depicting fish and other aquatic creatures. My works also take inspiration from comics, games, folklore, and silly things I see online, because my favourite kind of art is the kind that makes people smile and laugh. I’m experienced in designing for web and print, using a variety of tools such as Figma, the Adobe Suite, Protopie, and Procreate. Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
